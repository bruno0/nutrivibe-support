Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with NutriVibe?

Download the app, create an account or try guest mode, complete the onboarding to set your nutrition goals, and start tracking your meals.

How are my nutrition targets calculated?

NutriVibe uses AI to personalize your daily calorie and macro targets based on your age, biological sex, activity level, health goals, and dietary preferences.

Can I use the app without creating an account?

Yes! NutriVibe offers a guest mode so you can try the app with demo data before deciding to create an account.

Is my data private and secure?

Yes. Your nutrition data is stored securely. See our Privacy Policy for details.

How do I reset my password?

On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password" and enter your email. You'll receive a temporary password to log in and then change it in your profile settings.

What languages does NutriVibe support?

NutriVibe is available in English.

Is NutriVibe a medical app?

No. NutriVibe is a nutrition tracking tool designed to help you understand your eating patterns. It is not medical advice and should not replace consultation with healthcare professionals.
