Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 4, 2025

⚕️ Medical Disclaimer: NutriVibe provides nutritional information for educational purposes only and is NOT a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult with a qualified healthcare provider before making dietary changes.

1. Information We Collect

Required Information

To provide core nutrition calculation features, we require:

  • Account Information: Email address, name, password (encrypted)
  • Physical Measurements: Height and weight (required for nutrition calculations using the Mifflin-St Jeor equation)

Optional Information

The following information helps personalize your experience but is not required:

  • Date of birth - For age-appropriate nutrition recommendations
  • Biological sex - For more accurate metabolic rate calculations
  • Country - For regional food recommendations and seasonal produce suggestions
  • Ethnicity - For culturally appropriate meal suggestions

Usage Data

  • Nutrition tracking data (meals logged, calories, macros)
  • Activity and exercise logs
  • Health goals and preferences
  • Water intake tracking
  • Mood entries (if you choose to use this feature)
  • App usage statistics and crash reports

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Nutrition Calculations: Calculate personalized daily calorie and macronutrient targets using evidence-based formulas (Mifflin-St Jeor equation, TDEE calculations)
  • AI-Powered Recommendations: Generate meal suggestions and nutrition guidance through our AI service provider
  • Progress Tracking: Monitor your nutrition intake and achievement of health goals
  • App Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to improve features and user experience
  • Account Management: Send important account updates and notifications

3. Third-Party Services

AI Service Provider: We use Google Gemini API to process your nutrition data and generate personalized meal recommendations. When you use AI features:

  • Your profile data and nutrition preferences are transmitted securely to Google's servers
  • Data is used solely to generate your meal recommendations
  • Google does not store or retain your personal health data
  • Transmissions are encrypted using industry-standard protocols

Authentication: We offer social login through Google and Apple Sign-In. When you use these services, we only receive your email address and name - we never access your password.

4. Data Security

  • All data is encrypted in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest
  • Passwords are hashed using industry-standard bcrypt encryption
  • We use secure cloud infrastructure with regular security audits
  • Access to personal data is restricted to authorized personnel only
  • We never sell, rent, or share your personal health information with third parties for marketing purposes

5. Data Retention

  • Your account data is retained as long as your account is active
  • Nutrition tracking history is stored indefinitely unless you delete it
  • You can delete individual entries or your entire account at any time
  • After account deletion, your data is permanently removed within 30 days
  • Backup systems may retain encrypted copies for up to 90 days for disaster recovery

6. Your Rights (GDPR & Privacy Laws)

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: Update or correct your information in the app settings
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all associated data at any time
  • Withdraw Consent: Stop using optional features or delete your account
  • Object to Processing: Contact us to object to specific data uses

To exercise these rights, use the in-app settings or contact us at brunosony@outlook.com

8. Guest Mode

You can try NutriVibe using Guest Mode with demo data. Guest Mode data:

  • Is stored locally on your device only
  • Is not transmitted to our servers
  • Is deleted when you close the app or create an account

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notification. Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in accordance with this privacy policy and applicable laws.

11. Contact Us

Questions or concerns about privacy?

  • Email: brunosony@outlook.com
  • Response Time: We aim to respond within 48 hours

12. Consent

By creating an account and using NutriVibe, you consent to the collection and processing of your data as described in this privacy policy. You can withdraw consent at any time by deleting your account.
